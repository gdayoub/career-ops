- **R:** End-to-end running pipeline with real-time Streamlit dashboard showing threat scores and trend visualizations

- **T:** Translate SQL pipeline results and efficiency trend data into actionable reports for field engineers (not data scientists)

- **R:** Engineering teams adopted the reports into their standard QC workflow; feedback was that the narratives made the data "finally understandable"
